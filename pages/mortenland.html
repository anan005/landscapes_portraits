Morten Spaberg Portrait photographer

Morten Spaberg is a Norwegian portrait and landscape photographer. His primary interest is in documenting the different people and landscape around him. Spaberg’s photos range from colour, black and white, analogue and digital, while primarily adhering to a vertical format. Photography is perhaps most potent when it conveys something about the world in which we live and its different people and landscapes — that which would go unnoticed if we didn’t point a camera at it.
